## Responsible Use of AI Tools

### Understanding AI Tools in My Role

As a Product Manager Intern, AI tools can support many aspects of my work. These tools can help with research, documentation, idea generation, and communication. Some common AI tools that may be useful in my role include:

- **ChatGPT / Claude** for research support, drafting documentation, and brainstorming product ideas
- **Cursor or AI coding assistants** to understand code snippets or technical implementation details
- **AI writing assistants** to improve clarity in documentation or communication
- **AI summarisation tools** to quickly review long documents or meeting notes

While these tools can improve efficiency, they should always be used as **assistive tools rather than decision-makers**.

---

## Benefits and Risks of Using AI in a Professional Setting

### Benefits

AI tools can significantly improve productivity when used responsibly. Some key benefits include:

- **Faster research and information gathering**
- **Assistance with drafting documentation or reports**
- **Generating ideas or alternative perspectives**
- **Summarising complex information**
- **Supporting problem analysis**

For product management tasks, AI can help explore potential product improvements, organise ideas, and structure documentation more efficiently.

### Risks

Despite the benefits, there are also important risks associated with AI tools:

- **Data privacy risks** if confidential information is entered into AI systems
- **Incorrect or outdated information** generated by AI
- **Over-reliance on AI**, which may reduce critical thinking
- **Lack of context**, which can cause AI suggestions to be inappropriate for a specific product or company

Because of these risks, AI outputs should always be reviewed and validated carefully.

---

## Information That Should Never Be Entered into AI Tools

To protect company data and user privacy, certain types of information should never be shared with AI tools. These include:

- **Confidential company information**
- **User personal data or identifiable information**
- **Private internal documents or code repositories**
- **Sensitive business strategies or product plans**
- **Login credentials or security-related information**

Maintaining data confidentiality is especially important for Focus Bear, as the company handles sensitive information related to users’ habits, productivity patterns, and personal data.

---

## Fact-Checking and Validating AI-Generated Content

AI-generated content should always be reviewed critically before being used in professional work.

To ensure accuracy, I will:

- Verify information using **trusted sources or official documentation**
- Check whether AI responses **match the company’s context or product requirements**
- Review outputs for **logical consistency and clarity**
- Edit and refine AI-generated content to ensure it meets professional standards

AI should be seen as a **starting point for ideas**, not a final answer.

# Reflection

## When to Use AI vs. When to Rely on My Own Skills

AI is most useful when assisting with tasks such as:

- Researching general concepts
- Drafting initial documentation
- Brainstorming ideas
- Organising information

However, I should rely on my own skills when making decisions that require:

- Product judgement
- Understanding of user needs
- Context about the company’s strategy
- Ethical or privacy considerations

For example, AI may help generate ideas for improving a feature, but the final decision should be based on user feedback, product goals, and team discussion.

---

## Avoiding Over-Reliance on AI

To avoid becoming overly dependent on AI tools, I will:

- Use AI mainly as **a support tool rather than a primary solution**
- Apply **critical thinking** when reviewing AI outputs
- Combine AI assistance with **personal research and analysis**
- Ensure that I understand the reasoning behind the information provided

This approach allows me to benefit from AI while still developing my own professional skills.

---

## Protecting Data Privacy When Using AI

To ensure data privacy when using AI tools, I will follow these principles:

- Never enter **confidential company information** into AI tools
- Avoid sharing **personal or identifiable user data**
- Use AI primarily for **general knowledge and productivity assistance**
- Follow company policies regarding data security and privacy

Maintaining user trust is critical for any product, especially one focused on productivity and well-being like Focus Bear.
